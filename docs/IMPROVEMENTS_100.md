# TrustCart 100/100 Improvements Added

## Brand Positioning

TrustCart is now positioned as a verified, sustainable, and consumer-protection-focused e-commerce platform.

## Major Improvements

1. TrustCart Shield badge
2. Product Trust Score breakdown
3. Green Score per product
4. Seller Passport verification
5. Verified buyer review policy
6. Red flag review summary
7. Plastic-free packaging badge
8. Locally sourced badge
9. Low-waste delivery badge
10. Green checkout options
11. Eco delivery discount
12. Transparent true price display
13. Separate Seller Center
14. Admin seller approval
15. Admin product approval
16. Refund and protection center
17. Custom error page to avoid default Whitelabel screen

## Presentation Message

TrustCart is not just a Shopee copy. It is a safer and greener marketplace that only allows legitimate sellers to sell, shows trust and sustainability scores, protects buyers from fake reviews and fake items, and makes the final price clear before checkout.
