About TrustCart

A marketplace built around trust, sustainability, and buyer confidence.

TrustCart is designed as a verified green marketplace where approved sellers, safer checkout, nearby seller filtering, and buyer protection work together to improve the online shopping experience.

TrustCart Standard

✓ Verified sellers and products✓ Transparent checkout and protection reminders✓ Sustainability and location integrity features

What TrustCart Offers

Verified marketplace
Only approved sellers can offer products on the platform.

Green commerce
Product Green Score, eco-packaging, consolidated delivery, and local seller visibility support sustainability.

Buyer-first design
True price display, order tracking, refund center, and clear seller indicators help buyers make safer decisions.

Why It Is Different

Trust before volume
TrustCart prioritizes seller legitimacy and product reliability over simply increasing listings.

Protected location system
Seller exact addresses are verified internally but not exposed to buyers to reduce off-platform transactions.

Retention features
TrustPoints, discount codes, and autoship subscriptions encourage repeat use within the platform.

Continue shopping safely

All purchases, payments, refunds, TrustPoints, and support should stay inside TrustCart for protection.
