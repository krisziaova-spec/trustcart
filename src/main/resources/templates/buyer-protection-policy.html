Buyer Protection

How TrustCart helps buyers shop with confidence.

Buyer protection combines verified sellers, product transparency, protected checkout, order tracking, refund workflow, and anti-off-platform reminders.

TrustCart Standard

✓ Verified sellers and products✓ Transparent checkout and protection reminders✓ Sustainability and location integrity features

Protection Coverage

Verified sellers
Sellers must be approved before selling.

Product review
Products must follow TrustCart authenticity, prohibited-item, and sustainability rules before appearing in the marketplace.

Order support
Protected orders can be tracked and used for refund or return requests.

Not Covered

Off-platform purchases
Transactions completed outside TrustCart are not covered.

Incomplete evidence
Claims without order details or supporting evidence may be harder to resolve.

Buyer misuse
Abusive, fraudulent, or dishonest claims may be rejected.
