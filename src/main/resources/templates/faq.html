Buyer Protection

How does TrustCart protect buyers?

TrustCart uses verified sellers, protected checkout, order tracking, internal chat, support tickets, and admin review. Buyers should keep payment and communication inside TrustCart so concerns can be reviewed with evidence.

What is protected checkout?

Protected checkout means the order is recorded in TrustCart with item details, quantity, seller, fulfillment type, voucher use, payment simulation, and order status. This creates a trail for support if something goes wrong.

What should I do if I receive the wrong item?

Open a ticket through Support, attach a photo or file proof, and include the order/product details. Admin can review the concern and coordinate seller action, refund review, or escalation.

Can I transact outside TrustCart?

TrustCart discourages off-platform transactions. Orders outside TrustCart may not be covered by buyer protection, order tracking, tickets, or admin review.

TrustPoints and Rewards

How do TrustPoints work?

TrustPoints are rewards earned from completed TrustCart purchases. Buyers earn 1 point for every ₱100 purchase, and 1 point is equivalent to ₱1 discount at checkout.

When do I earn points?

Points are credited after the buyer confirms received delivery. This prevents points from being granted on cancelled, refunded, or unpaid orders.

Can points be removed?

Yes. If an order is refunded, cancelled, reversed, or found abusive, TrustCart can adjust the point balance to protect the platform.

What are loyalty tiers?

Loyalty tiers recognize repeat buyers. Higher tiers may unlock better vouchers, priority support, or exclusive promos in future versions.

Orders and Fulfillment

What is Fulfilled by Seller?

The seller keeps stock, prepares the order, and handles delivery or pickup coordination. TrustCart still records the order and provides ticket/report support.

What is Fulfilled by TrustCart?

The seller sends stock to a TrustCart warehouse or hub. TrustCart verifies stock, stores inventory, packs orders, and handles fulfillment. Sellers cannot self-declare FBT; admin approval is required.

How do I track my order?

Go to My Orders or Track Order. Order statuses may include To Pay, To Ship, To Receive, Completed, Cancelled, and Return/Refund.

When can I review a product?

You can review only after purchasing the product and confirming received delivery. This keeps reviews limited to verified purchases.

Chat, Tickets, Reviews, and Safety

Can I chat with a seller?

Yes. Use Contact Seller or Messages. You can attach files or photos when proof is needed, such as product questions, delivery proof, receipts, or screenshots.

How do I report a seller or product?

Use Report Seller, Report Product, or the Support Contact form. Attach proof when available so admin can investigate faster.

Are bad reviews removed?

No. Honest negative reviews are allowed. Reviews are only hidden or removed after admin investigation if they are proven fake, abusive, unrelated, competitor-driven, or not tied to a verified purchase.

What happens to risky accounts?

Admin can place buyers or stores under review, request valid ID and face verification, restrict accounts, deactivate stores, or block users when repeated valid reports or serious concerns are confirmed.
