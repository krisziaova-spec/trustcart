Buyer Questions

How does TrustCart protect buyers?

TrustCart requires buyer login for cart, checkout, tracking, and refund requests. It also displays seller verification, product Trust Score, Green Score, review summary, true price breakdown, and order protection reminders.

Can I search for nearby sellers?

Yes. Use Target Market Location on the shop page to select a city, radius, nearby seller filter, and pickup-capable seller filter. This helps prioritize faster delivery and pickup options.

Why is the seller exact address hidden?

TrustCart shows only the public seller area and protected pickup label. This reduces off-platform transactions and protects both buyers and legitimate sellers.

What happens if I transact outside TrustCart?

Orders made outside TrustCart are not covered by TrustCart order tracking, refund support, buyer protection, TrustPoints, discounts, or dispute assistance.

What payment options are available?

Checkout includes Cash on Delivery, GCash Payment, Maya Payment, Credit/Debit Card, and Bank Transfer options.

How do discount codes work?

Enter an active discount code on checkout. The final total updates based on the code rules, item total, and other eligible discounts.

How do TrustPoints work?

Buyers earn TrustPoints on successful purchases. Points may be redeemed during checkout depending on available balance and redemption rules.

Seller Questions

Can anyone sell on TrustCart?

No. Sellers must apply and receive approval before posting products. Admin verification helps reduce fake items, fake stores, and misleading listings.

Why must sellers provide exact store location?

Exact location is used for admin verification, delivery planning, pickup eligibility, and nearby seller filtering. Buyers only see the approved public location label.

Can sellers upload product photos?

Yes. Sellers can add product photo URLs when creating product listings, making the marketplace more attractive and easier to browse.

Can sellers offer subscriptions or autoshipment?

Yes. Eligible repeat-use products, such as household supplies and personal care items, can support autoshipment with preferred shipment frequency.

What makes a seller more trustworthy?

Verified business profile, accurate location, approved product records, low complaint rate, reliable fulfillment, sustainable packaging, and consistent buyer satisfaction improve seller trust.

Sustainability Questions

How does TrustCart support sustainability?

TrustCart highlights Green Score, eco-friendly product indicators, plastic-free packaging, locally sourced products, consolidated delivery, and autoshipment planning to reduce waste and improve fulfillment efficiency.

What is Green Checkout?

Green Checkout allows buyers to select eco-packaging, no extra plastic, and consolidated eco delivery options where available.

What is a Green Score?

Green Score summarizes product and seller sustainability indicators such as packaging type, sourcing claims, eco-friendly materials, and delivery options.

How does pickup help sustainability?

Pickup through a protected hub can reduce delivery trips, support faster fulfillment, and keep transaction protection inside the platform.
