Off-Platform Safety

Why buyers and sellers must stay inside TrustCart.

TrustCart protects orders only when shopping, payment selection, tracking, refunds, and communication remain within the platform flow.

TrustCart Standard

✓ Verified sellers and products✓ Transparent checkout and protection reminders✓ Sustainability and location integrity features

Why It Matters

Buyer protection
TrustCart cannot verify or support transactions made outside the platform.

Refund support
Refund requests need official order records.

Rewards and discounts
TrustPoints and discount codes only apply to TrustCart checkout orders.

Seller Location Protection

Exact address hidden
Buyers see only a public location label or pickup hub label.

Seller verification
Exact location is used internally for verification and fulfillment planning.

Protected pickup
Pickup should happen through TrustCart-approved pickup flow, not direct buyer-seller arrangement.

Warning Signs

Direct payment request
Avoid sellers asking for bank transfer or wallet payment outside checkout.

Direct meetup request
Avoid sellers asking to meet outside protected pickup flow.

External contact push
Avoid transactions moved to private messaging to bypass TrustCart records.

Continue shopping safely

All purchases, payments, refunds, TrustPoints, and support should stay inside TrustCart for protection.
