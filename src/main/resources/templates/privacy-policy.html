Privacy Policy

How TrustCart handles user and seller information.

This page explains the basic privacy practices used in the TrustCart platform experience.

TrustCart Standard

✓ Verified sellers and products✓ Transparent checkout and protection reminders✓ Sustainability and location integrity features

Information We Collect

Account data
Name, email, phone number, buyer address, seller business details, and login details may be collected for account use.

Transaction data
Orders, cart actions, payment selection, discount codes, TrustPoints, subscriptions, and refund requests may be recorded.

Location data
Buyer target market location and seller exact store location may be used for nearby search, delivery planning, and verification.

How We Use Information

Platform operation
Data is used to operate shopping, checkout, seller approval, order tracking, refunds, and support features.

Safety and verification
Seller location and product data support legitimacy checks and buyer protection.

Retention features
Purchase data supports TrustPoints, discounts, and autoshipment settings.

Protection Practices

Limited seller address exposure
Buyer-facing pages show public seller area only, not exact store address.

Internal use
Exact seller location is intended for seller verification and fulfillment logic.

User responsibility
Users should not share sensitive payment or identity information through public messages.

Continue shopping safely

All purchases, payments, refunds, TrustPoints, and support should stay inside TrustCart for protection.
