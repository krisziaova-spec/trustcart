Prohibited Items

Items not allowed on TrustCart.

This policy helps protect buyers, sellers, and the platform from unsafe, illegal, counterfeit, or misleading products.

Not Allowed

Counterfeit goods
Fake branded items and misleading replicas are not allowed.

Unsafe items
Products that may harm buyers or violate safety standards are not allowed.

Illegal products
Products prohibited by applicable law or platform rules are not allowed.

Deceptive listings
Listings with fake photos, false claims, or hidden conditions are not allowed.

Restricted Review

High-risk categories
Certain items such as electronics, beauty products, supplements, and baby products may require closer review.

Sustainability claims
Eco claims must be clear and not misleading.

Location claims
Virtual or fake store locations are not allowed.

Enforcement

Product rejection
Listings that violate platform rules may be removed.

Listing removal
Approved products may be removed after reports or review.

Seller restriction
Seller accounts may be suspended for repeated violations.

Continue shopping safely

All purchases, payments, refunds, TrustPoints, and support should stay inside TrustCart for protection.
