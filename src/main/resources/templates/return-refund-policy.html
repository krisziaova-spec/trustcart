Returns and Refunds

Clear steps for resolving order concerns.

TrustCart supports a structured Protection Center workflow for return and refund concerns.

TrustCart Standard

✓ Verified sellers and products✓ Transparent checkout and protection reminders✓ Sustainability and location integrity features

Eligible Concerns

Wrong item
Buyer received a different item from the approved listing.

Damaged item
Item arrived damaged or unusable with reasonable evidence.

Missing item
Order was incomplete or product was not delivered as expected.

Authenticity concern
Buyer suspects counterfeit or misleading product representation.

Required Evidence

Order reference
Buyer should provide order code and product details.

Photos or video
Evidence may include item photo, packaging, waybill, and defect proof.

Clear explanation
Buyer should explain the problem and desired resolution.

Resolution Options

Refund
Full or partial refund may be reviewed depending on issue and evidence.

Replacement
Replacement may be offered when stock and seller approval are available.

Local resolution
For minor concerns, TrustCart may encourage digital evidence review first to reduce unnecessary reverse logistics.

Continue shopping safely

All purchases, payments, refunds, TrustPoints, and support should stay inside TrustCart for protection.
