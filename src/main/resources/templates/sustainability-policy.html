Sustainability Policy

How TrustCart promotes greener online shopping.

TrustCart includes sustainability features that help buyers choose greener products and delivery options.

TrustCart Standard

✓ Verified sellers and products✓ Transparent checkout and protection reminders✓ Sustainability and location integrity features

Green Marketplace Tools

Green Score
Products may show a sustainability score based on packaging, material, sourcing, and fulfillment indicators.

Eco badges
Listings may display badges such as plastic-free packaging, locally sourced, reusable product, or low-waste packaging.

Green checkout
Buyers may select eco-packaging, no extra plastic, or eco consolidated delivery where available.

Local Commerce

Nearby sellers
Target market filtering helps buyers find closer sellers.

Local products
Local Filipino products and MSMEs can receive clearer visibility.

Reduced fulfillment waste
Subscriptions and consolidated delivery can improve planning and reduce repeated fragmented shipments.

Anti-greenwashing

Claims should be accurate
Sellers should not exaggerate environmental benefits.

Seller accountability review
Sustainability claims may be reviewed before badges are shown.

Buyer transparency
Badges and scores should help buyers understand the basis of green claims.

Continue shopping safely

All purchases, payments, refunds, TrustPoints, and support should stay inside TrustCart for protection.
