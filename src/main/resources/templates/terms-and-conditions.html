Rules for using TrustCart responsibly.

These terms explain the expected conduct for buyers, sellers, and platform users.

TrustCart Standard

✓ Verified sellers and products✓ Transparent checkout and protection reminders✓ Sustainability and location integrity features

General Use

Account responsibility
Users are responsible for accurate information and safe account use.

Platform transactions
Orders, payments, refunds, TrustPoints, and subscriptions must be processed within TrustCart to remain protected.

Misuse prohibited
Fraud, fake reviews, misleading products, duplicate accounts, and abusive activity are not allowed.

Buyer Responsibilities

Review product information
Buyers should check product details, seller indicators, Trust Score, Green Score, and total price before checkout.

Use official channels
Buyers should use cart, checkout, tracking, and Protection Center instead of off-platform arrangements.

Evidence for claims
Refund and return requests may require photos, order details, and reasonable proof.

Seller Responsibilities

Accurate listings
Sellers must provide truthful product details, price, stock, photos, fulfillment capability, and sustainability claims.

Location integrity
Sellers must provide a real store or fulfillment location for verification.

No circumvention
Sellers must not push buyers to transact outside TrustCart.

Continue shopping safely

All purchases, payments, refunds, TrustPoints, and support should stay inside TrustCart for protection.
